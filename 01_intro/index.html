Week 1: Circuit Boards


This week we got to learn how to add components to breadboards. We specifically used a microcontroller, wires, a resistor, and an LED in circuit to get the LED to turn on. We used the 3V output from the microcontroller, but also played with the bat (battery) output, which had less voltage and a dimmer light, and the 5V USB output to get a brigher light.
